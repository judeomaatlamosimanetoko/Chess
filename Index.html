About Mateo

Grandmaster Mateo Kovac (b. 1992) earned his title at 19, becoming the youngest GM in his country’s history. With three Olympiad medals and a peak rating of 2658, he has competed against World Championship contenders and trained rising talents from 1200 to 2400 Elo.

Beyond competitive play, Mateo is the author of "Dynamic Endgame Blueprint" and a regular lecturer at top chess academies. His training philosophy blends classical principles with modern engine-assisted refinement.

Available for private lessons, simultaneous exhibitions, and team analysis.
